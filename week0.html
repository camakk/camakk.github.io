what I made: omelet-making kitchen

To teach a machine to prepare an omelet

Omelets are a staple of any meal-plan-less college diet. My idea here is to build a machine to make omelets, but focus specifically on designing the device to be desirable. This isn't a steam engine factory machine that pumps out omelets - this is a high-end classy device that makes omelets with care and grace.

The material selection will be extremely varied and the overall size is still indeterminate, but it could range anywhere from a table-top contraption to a full on vending machine sized device. I will add more details as this idea is further developed.
